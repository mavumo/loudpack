Blue Gelato, also known as "Blue Gelato #41," is a deliciously sweet hybrid marijuana strain made by crossing DJ Short’s old school Blueberry with GSCand Sherbert.



With so many delicious strains at play, Blue Gelato puts out a smooth earthy, citrus, and fruity terpene profile that tastes as good as it smells.

As for the high, you can expect to feel lofty and free in a state of euphoric bliss.
